HOTEL SHARADA INTERNATIONAL

Sharada International in Udupi is a casual hotel offering a budget-friendly stay for travelers. The hotel boasts unpretentious rooms and suites that are clean and comfortable, featuring basic amenities like air conditioning and TVs. Guest reviews mention the rooms being well-maintained, with some highlighting the excellent service provided by the friendly and helpful staff.

While breakfast might not be the most elaborate spread, guests commend the in-house restaurant for serving delicious and mouthwatering dishes, particularly praising items like chicken 65, sizzler chicken, and chicken ghee roast. The hotel also features a bar-cum-restaurant downstairs and a rooftop terrace, offering additional dining options and a relaxing space to unwind. Overall, Sharada International seems like a good option for budget-conscious travelers seeking a comfortable stay in Udupi.
