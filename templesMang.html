Kadri Shree Manjunatha Temple

The Kadri Shree Manjunatha Temple, located atop the Kadri Hill in Mangalore, Karnataka, is one of the oldest and most revered Hindu temples in the region. Dedicated to Lord Manjunatha, a manifestation of Lord Shiva, this temple holds immense religious significance and attracts devotees from far and wide.

Kudroli Gokarnath Temple

The Kudroli Gokarnath Temple, situated in Mangalore, Karnataka, is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Gokarnatheshwara, a form of Lord Shiva. Established by Narayana Guru, a prominent social reformer and spiritual leader, the temple holds significant religious and cultural importance in the region.

ISKCON Temple Mangalore

The ISKCON (International Society for Krishna Consciousness) Temple in Mangalore is a vibrant center of spiritual and cultural activities dedicated to Lord Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Situated in the heart of the city, this temple is a focal point for devotees and visitors seeking spiritual enrichment and cultural immersion.

Sri Mangaladevi Temple, Mangalore

The Sri Mangaladevi Temple in Mangalore, Karnataka, is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Mangaladevi, the presiding deity of the city from which Mangalore derives its name. Situated in the heart of the city, this ancient temple holds immense religious significance and cultural heritage.
