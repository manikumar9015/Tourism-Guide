Hotel Manorama

Established in 1988, Hotel Manorama has carved a niche for itself in Mangalore's hospitality sector. Catering primarily to budget-conscious travelers, the hotel offers a convenient location in the heart of the city, right next to the City Center Mall. This central position makes it an ideal base for exploring Mangalore's vibrant streets, bustling markets, and historical landmarks.

The hotel's facade might not win any design awards, but step inside and you'll be greeted by a surprisingly charming reception area. Adorned with antique sculptures and traditional wooden artifacts, the lobby hints at the hotel's blend of practicality and local character. Rooms at Hotel Manorama are simple yet functional, offering air-conditioning, clean linens, and comfortable beds. While some reviewers have mentioned the occasional concerns about dated decor, most guests find the rooms perfectly adequate for a short stay. A consistent positive aspect highlighted in reviews is the availability of 24/7 hot water, a welcome amenity, especially for those unfamiliar with the region.

For those seeking a luxurious stay with all the trimmings, Hotel Manorama might not be the perfect fit. However, for travelers prioritizing location, comfort, and affordability, this hotel offers a compelling proposition. Whether you're a solo adventurer, a couple on a shoestring budget, or a group exploring the coastal city, Hotel Manorama provides a no-frills yet pleasant haven in the heart of Mangalore.
