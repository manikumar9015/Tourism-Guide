Chaithrapura Beach

Chaithrapura Beach, nestled along the captivating coastline of Mangalore, offers visitors a serene escape into nature's embrace. Surrounded by lush greenery and swaying palm trees, Chaithrapura Beach exudes an aura of tranquility, inviting visitors to unwind and soak in the peaceful ambiance.

Panamburu Beach

Panambur Beach, situated near Mangalore in Karnataka, India, is a popular coastal destination known for its pristine sandy shores and tranquil ambiance. Offering breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea, it attracts visitors with its inviting waters and opportunities for water sports such as jet skiing and banana boat rides. The beach is also renowned for its cleanliness and well-maintained facilities.
