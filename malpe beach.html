Malpe Beach

Nestled on the Karnataka coast, Malpe beach in Udupi district offers a captivating blend of relaxation and adventure. The golden sands stretch for miles, meeting the turquoise waters of the Arabian Sea in a mesmerizing display. Gentle waves lap at the shore, creating a soothing soundtrack for a day of unwinding under the warm Indian sun.

Beyond the serene atmosphere, Malpe teems with vibrant life. Colourful fishing boats bob on the water, their nets promising a fresh catch for the local markets. Adventure seekers can partake in a thrilling array of water sports, from parasailing and jet skiing to banana boat rides. Ferries depart regularly for St. Mary's Island, a rocky outcrop known for its unique geological formations and diverse marine life.
