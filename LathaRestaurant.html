Hotel Latha

Hotel Latha, though not exactly an inn, is a well-known vegetarian restaurant in Kota, Udupi. Established over 60 years ago, it's a favorite among locals and travelers alike. They're renowned for their delicious and authentic Udupi cuisine, prepared using family recipes passed down through generations.

Their menu boasts a wide range of South Canara vegetarian fare, including the famed menthe dosa, ragi mudde with bassaru, shavige, and moode. The restaurant is known for its simple ambience and pocket-friendly prices, making it a great choice for a satisfying and affordable meal. While Hotel Latha itself may not offer lodging, there are likely hotels or guesthouses nearby that you can explore for your stay in Kota.
