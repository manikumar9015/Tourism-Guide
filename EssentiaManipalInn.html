Essentia Manipal Inn

Essentia Manipal Inn, positioned as the largest hotel in its class in Manipal, offers a luxurious stay experience for travelers visiting the temple town of Udupi or the educational hub of Manipal. Boasting a 4.5 star rating on Google with over 3000 reviews, the hotel is known for its spacious and well-maintained rooms, impeccable cleanliness, and courteous staff. Guests can choose from 30 deluxe rooms and 4 suites, all equipped with modern amenities for a comfortable stay.

Beyond comfortable accommodations, Essentia Manipal Inn stands out for its diverse dining options and banquet halls. The hotel features multiple restaurants serving a variety of cuisines, from vegetarian fare to delicious seafood dishes. This makes it a great choice for both business and leisure travelers, offering a convenient solution for hosting meetings, conferences, or social gatherings of all sizes. With its well-appointed function halls and delectable culinary offerings, Essentia Manipal Inn strives to provide a memorable and hassle-free experience for its guests.
