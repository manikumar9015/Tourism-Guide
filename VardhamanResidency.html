Vardhaman Residency

Established in the year 2010, Vardhaman Residency has become a familiar name in Mangalore's budget-friendly hospitality scene. Situated in the Yeyyadi Industrial Area, this hotel offers a convenient location for those visiting the city for business or leisure. While not in the heart of the tourist district, Vardhaman Residency provides easy access to various parts of Mangalore through a well-connected network of public transportation.

Step inside the hotel, and you might encounter a welcoming reception area that prioritizes functionality over grandeur. The friendly staff, known for their helpfulness, are readily available to assist you with check-in, answer your queries, and perhaps even offer recommendations for exploring the city. Guest rooms at Vardhaman Residency focus on providing a comfortable and clean space for a relaxing stay. Expect standard amenities like air-conditioning, comfortable beds with fresh linens, and a television with cable channels for entertainment. While the decor might be slightly dated as per some online reviews, most guests find the rooms perfectly adequate for a restful night's sleep after a day of exploring Mangalore's vibrant offerings.

Beyond the confines of your room, the amenities offered by Vardhaman Residency cater to a comfortable and hassle-free stay. Some reviews mention an in-house restaurant serving delicious South Indian cuisine, a perfect introduction to the region's culinary heritage. For those seeking a wider variety, the surrounding Yeyyadi neighborhood offers a plethora of restaurants and eateries catering to diverse tastes and budgets. From local favorites serving authentic Mangalore fare to familiar fast-food chains, you'll have a variety of options to choose from. The hotel's location near the Yeyyadi Industrial Area might also be convenient for business travelers with meetings or work commitments in that area.

Vardhaman Residency might not be the most luxurious option in Mangalore, but it offers a compelling proposition for budget-conscious travelers. With its convenient location, clean rooms, friendly staff, and potential in-house dining option, Vardhaman Residency provides a comfortable and hassle-free base for exploring the coastal city of Mangalore. Whether you're a solo adventurer, a couple on a budget, or a group exploring the city, Vardhaman Residency offers a welcoming haven in Mangalore.
