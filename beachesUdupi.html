Mattu Beach

Mattu Beach is a secluded beach located about 10 kilometers from the city of Udupi, India. It is a perfect getaway for those looking for relaxation and peace amidst natural surroundings. The beach is known for its mesmerizing views, calm backwaters, and awe-inspiring sunsets.

Maravanthe Beach

Maravanthe Beach in Karnataka, India, is famous for its stunning location where the Arabian Sea meets the Souparnika River. With its long stretch of golden sands, clear blue waters, and beautiful sunsets, it's a popular spot for relaxation and water sports. Surrounded by the Kodachadri Hills, the beach offers a tranquil atmosphere, perfect for leisurely strolls and serene moments by the sea.

Kapu Beach

Kapu Beach, located near Udupi in Karnataka, India, is a serene coastal spot celebrated for its golden sands and tranquil atmosphere. With panoramic vistas of the vast Arabian Sea, it beckons visitors with its calm waters and opportunities for engaging in water sports like surfing and boating. Kapu Beach is also famed for its picturesque lighthouse, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
