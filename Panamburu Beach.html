Panambur Beach

Panambur Beach, situated on the shores of the Arabian Sea in Mangalore, Karnataka, is a popular destination for both tourists and locals. It's known for its clean, soft sand and picturesque sunsets. Lifeguards patrol the beach to ensure visitor safety, making it a great place to relax and soak up the sun. The beach is well-maintained by a private enterprise and offers a variety of activities, including jet skiing, boating, and dolphin watching. Food stalls serving local delicacies line the shore, tempting visitors with delicious treats.

Beyond the leisure activities, Panambur Beach boasts a historical lighthouse offering stunning views of the coastline. The beach also comes alive during festivals organized by the local authorities, featuring exciting boat races, beach sports, and sand sculpture contests. Whether you seek relaxation, adventure, or cultural immersion, Panambur Beach has something to offer everyone.
