Udupi Shri Krishna Matha

The Udupi Shri Krishna Matha stands as a revered pilgrimage site and a symbol of devotion in Udupi, Karnataka. Dedicated to Lord Krishna, it holds immense spiritual significance for followers of the Vaishnavism tradition. The Matha, or monastery, is believed to have been founded by the 13th-century saint and philosopher Sri Madhvacharya.

Siddhi Vinayaka Temple Anegudde

The Siddhi Vinayaka Temple in Anegudde, Karnataka, is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. Situated amidst the picturesque surroundings of Anegudde village, this temple holds immense spiritual significance and attracts devotees from far and wide.

Shree Mahalakshmi Devasthana

The Shree Mahalakshmi Devasthana in Uchila, Karnataka, is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, the goddess of wealth, prosperity, and auspiciousness. Situated in the serene surroundings of Uchila village, this temple holds a special place in the hearts of devotees who come to seek the blessings of the divine goddess.

Sri Anantha Padmanabha Temple

The Sri Anantha Padmanabha Temple in Hebri, Karnataka, is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Anantha Padmanabha, a form of Lord Vishnu. Nestled amidst the lush greenery of Hebri village, this temple holds significant religious importance.

Kollur Temple

The Kollur Mookambika Temple, situated in Kollur, Karnataka, is one of the most revered Hindu temples dedicated to Goddess Mookambika, an incarnation of Goddess Parvati. Nestled amidst the picturesque Western Ghats, this ancient temple holds immense spiritual significance and attracts devotees from all over the country.
