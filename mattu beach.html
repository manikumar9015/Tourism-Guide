Mattu Beach

Mattu Beach, also known as Maravanthe Beach, is a stunning coastline located near Udupi in Karnataka, India. It's renowned for its unique geographical marvel - a beautiful stretch of white sand beach fringed by swaying palm trees on one side and bordered by the crystal-clear waters of the Arabian Sea on the other. The beach is further enhanced by the presence of the enchanting Colada River, which flows parallel to the coastline for a considerable distance, creating a mesmerizing spectacle of sand, sea, and river merging into one another

Mattu Beach offers a plethora of activities for visitors seeking a relaxing or adventurous getaway. One can soak up the sun on the pristine sands, indulge in refreshing dips in the sea, or embark on exciting boat rides on the Colada River. The beach is also a popular spot for water sports enthusiasts, with options like jet skiing, parasailing, and banana boat rides available. For those seeking a more serene experience, nature walks along the shore or boat trips to spot dolphins in their natural habitat are perfect choices.
