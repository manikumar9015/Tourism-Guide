Maravanthe Beach

Maravanthe Beach, also known as Virgin Beach, is a stunning coastal gem in Karnataka's Udupi district. Its unique charm lies in its dramatic landscape. On one side, the endless expanse of the Arabian Sea crashes against the shore, while the serene Suparnika River flows gracefully on the other. National Highway 66 winds picturesquely between these two bodies of water, creating a visual treat for travelers. Maravanthe's long stretch of pristine white sand and swaying palm trees beckons visitors for a relaxing retreat. The beach is particularly popular for its breathtaking sunsets, where the sky ignites in a riot of colors as the sun dips below the horizon.

Beyond the scenic beauty, Maravanthe offers a glimpse into the local life. You can witness traditional fishing practices by watching fishermen set sail in their colorful catamarans. For a touch of history, visit the Maraswamy Temple located across the road from the beach. With its captivating scenery and laid-back vibes, Maravanthe Beach is an ideal destination for those seeking a tranquil escape on the Karnataka coast.
