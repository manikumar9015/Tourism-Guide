Woodland Restaurant

Woodlands Restaurant, established in 1988, is a cornerstone of Udupi's culinary scene. Nestled near the iconic Sri Krishna Matha Temple in Car Street, Woodlands has been a popular destination for both locals and tourists seeking a taste of authentic South Indian fare. The unassuming exterior opens into a vibrant space, bustling with activity throughout the day. The aroma of freshly ground spices fills the air, whetting appetites and promising a delightful dining experience.

Woodlands offers a delectable vegetarian menu that caters to a variety of tastes. Their breakfast spread is legendary, featuring fluffy idlis dipped in tangy sambar and coconut chutney, crispy masala dosas served with piping hot filter coffee, and the melt-in-your-mouth delicacy, the banana bun. Beyond breakfast, the restaurant shines with an extensive lunch and dinner menu. Sample the comforting simplicity of a traditional Udupi meal (thali) with its perfect combination of rice, sambar, rasam, and curries. For those seeking a more elaborate experience, Woodlands boasts an impressive selection of dosas, uttapams, and vada served with an array of chutneys and accompaniments. Don't miss their signature Ghee Masala Dosa, a crispy crepe generously filled with a spiced potato masala and drenched in clarified butter.
