Hotel Surya

Hotel Surya Mangalore, established in 1983, caters to budget-conscious travelers seeking a convenient location in the city. Situated just 1 kilometer from Mangalore Central Railway Station and within easy reach of the bus stand, Hotel Surya offers a hassle-free starting point for exploring the city. While not in the absolute city center, its proximity to Hampankatta, a bustling commercial area, provides access to numerous shops, restaurants, and historical sites like St. Aloysius Chapel and Milagres Church.

The hotel itself is a modest property with a straightforward exterior. Rooms come in double and triple configurations, with both air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned options available. While modern amenities might be limited, guest reviews often mention the rooms being spacious and well-maintained. For those seeking to connect with loved ones back home, free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Beyond the guest rooms, Hotel Surya offers a few additional conveniences. A well-manicured garden provides a tranquil space to relax, and a terrace allows guests to enjoy the fresh air. For those traveling by car, free parking is available on-site. While the hotel doesn't have its own restaurant, room service is offered, and the surrounding area is packed with eateries serving a variety of cuisines. The friendly staff can also assist with arranging airport transfers or booking tours, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free stay

Hotel Surya might not be the most luxurious option in Mangalore, but it strikes a good balance between affordability and functionality. Its location makes it a convenient choice for explorers and business travelers alike. If you prioritize a clean and comfortable room in a strategic location, with friendly service to boot, then Hotel Surya is definitely worth considering for your trip to Mangalore.
